Luo-Style Ugali & Fish

A Lake Victoria delicacy

This traditional Luo meal from Western Kenya features soft ugali paired with tilapia from Lake Victoria, prepared with indigenous spices and cooking methods passed down through generations.

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 40 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

For the Ugali:

  • 4 cups maize flour (preferably Jogoo or Pembe brand)
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt

For the Fish:

  • 2 whole tilapia (about 500g each), cleaned
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp Luo fish masala (or mix of coriander, cumin, turmeric)
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 cups chopped kunde (cowpea leaves) or spinach
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Fish:

    Make 3 diagonal cuts on each side of the fish. Rub with lemon juice and salt, then set aside for 15 minutes.

  2. Make the Ugali:

    Bring water to boil in a sufuria. Gradually add maize flour while stirring continuously with a wooden spoon (mwiko). Reduce heat and continue stirring until the mixture thickens. Cover and let cook for 10 minutes on low heat.

  3. Cook the Fish:

    Heat oil in a pan. Sauté onions until golden. Add garlic, ginger, and spices, cooking for 1 minute until fragrant. Add tomatoes and cook until softened.

  4. Add the fish and enough water to partially cover. Simmer for 15 minutes, carefully turning once. Add greens in the last 5 minutes.

  5. Serve:

    Mold ugali into round balls using wet hands. Place on a wooden board (oruta) with fish and greens. Eat with hands as per tradition - use ugali to scoop fish and sauce.

Treezer's Tips

Traditional Touch

For authentic flavor, use smoked fish (omena) instead of fresh tilapia. Soak overnight to remove excess salt.

Serving Style

Luo traditionally serve this on a large wooden platter for communal eating. Try using banana leaves as plates!

Modern Twist

Add a tablespoon of peanut butter to the fish sauce for a richer, nuttier flavor common in Western Kenya.

Nutritional Information

Per serving: 650 calories | 45g protein | 75g carbs | 15g fat | Good source of Omega-3, Iron, and Vitamin A
